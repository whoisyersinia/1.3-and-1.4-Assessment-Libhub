Accepting books:

  1. Click the 'Books Lended' tab on the sidebar on the left
  2. You will see a list of the books you've lended. Here you can edit or delete books. Click on check status on the book you want to be borrowed.
  3. If anyone has requested to borrow a book, a list of the borrowers will be displayed. Here you can accept or deny borrowers.
  4. Once you've found a borrower to lend, click accept to see finalise the transaction.
  5. The borrower can now see you've accepted their borrow request.
  6. It is your reponsibilty to deliver the book to the borower.
  7. You cannot accept borrowers who already borrowing books

Borrowing books:

  1. Borrow books from the library menu in the home section OR search in the front page.
  2. Click borrow this book and insert required personal details.
  3. The lender of the book will be notifed and can accpet or possibly deny or request. See its progress in the 'Books borrowed' tab
    on the sidebar on the left. You must request for one book to have access to this page.
  4. Books will be seperated from 'pending requests' to 'borrowing now'. Here you will be able to keep track of books you've requested and borrowing.
  5. Once your request has been approved. you can see when to return the book (mostly 14 days after approval).
  6. When you've finished borrowing, click return book and the lender will be notifed that you've finished borrowing.
  7. It is your responsibilty to return the book back to the lender.
  8. You only borrow one book at a time! But have multiple requests
